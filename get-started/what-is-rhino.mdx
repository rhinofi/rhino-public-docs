---
title: What is rhino.fi?
---
Rhino.fi is stablecoin onboarding infrastructure built for global scale. We provide universal deposit addresses that settle exactly where you need them — vault-ready, card-ready, or trading-ready in under 10 seconds.

Rhino takes care of stablecoin onboarding end-to-end: from deposit and routing to settlement, compliance, and automated on-chain activation. Deposits become usable balances instantly with no manual steps.

Our system is powered by collateralized bridges and liquidity across 20+ chains, with deterministic settlement and 99.99% route success. Every transfer is 100% KYT-screened with audit-ready logs, and error handling — wrong chains, failures, refunds — is all automated behind the scenes.

Go live in days with our API and expand into new markets without rebuilding your stack.
